INSTRUCTIONS: Use the 'A' key to move left. Use the 'D' key to move right. Press the spacebar to jump. Can you use it twice?

Press numpad key '1' to fire your gun, '2' to dash, '5' to slash, and '6' to cleave.

Holding 'W' while shooting or slashing will use energy for a more powerful version: Dash always costs 1 energy. Energized Shot costs 2 energy. Energized Slash costs 4 (this is your get out of jail free card!).

Dash has a brief window of invulnerability. Use it wisely!

Everything has a counter! Try stuff out! Press F5 when you die. (Make sure you have a working F5).

***HINTS/SPOILERS***

***HINTS/SPOILERS*** Crows require two hits from anything to kill. Dino's energy balls can be destroyed by shooting them. Soldier's bullets take 3 shots to destroy, but a single slash will finish them off nice and quick. You won't do damage to Soldier if you attack his shield. Find another way!
